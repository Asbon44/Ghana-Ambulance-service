First Aid Guide

CPR (Adults)

1. Request an ambulance immediately.

2. Push hard and fast in the center of the chest.

3. 100-120 compressions per minute.

Choking

1. Give 5 back blows between shoulder blades.

2. Give 5 abdominal thrusts (Heimlich maneuver).

3. Repeat until the object is forced out.

Severe Bleeding

1. Apply direct pressure with a clean cloth.

2. Keep pressure until help arrives.

3. If blood soaks through, add more cloth on top.

Burns

1. Run cool (not cold) water over the burn for 10-20 mins.

2. Cover with a sterile, non-adhesive bandage.
